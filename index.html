F.L.A.W. is an outdoor furry fandom weekend with exclusive access to Camp Whitman in Penn Yan, NY. Participants stay in cabins, are fed 6 delicious meals, and can enjoy activities including canoeing in Seneca Lake, swimming, fishing, games, campfires, crafting, and more. We hope to provide a fun weekend to relax, enjoy nature, and make memories together!

We are a volunteer-run, non-profit organization officially founded in 2026. With every event we raise money for charity and hope you can join us in raising funds to help good causes.

Ages 14+ only (minors must attend with a parent or legal guardian)
