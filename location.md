## Camp Whitman on Seneca Lake

**150 Whitman Rd, Penn Yan, NY 14527**

## Getting There

Driving is the primary way to get to Camp Whitman. It's an easy drive from most of upstate New York and the surrounding states.

**Nearest airports:**

- **ELM** (Elmira Corning), ~45 minutes drive
- **ITH** (Ithaca Tompkins), ~1 hour drive
- **ROC** (Rochester), ~1.5 hours drive
- **SYR** (Syracuse), ~1.5 hours drive
