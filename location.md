## Camp Whitman on Seneca Lake

**150 Whitman Rd, Penn Yan, NY**

<img src="/assets/programming_fur_1.png" alt="F.L.A.W. mascot canoeing" class="float-end ms-3 mb-3" style="max-height: 220px;">

F.L.A.W. takes place at Camp Whitman, right on the shore of Seneca Lake in New York's Finger Lakes region. Green fields, tall trees, cabins, and direct lake access. All exclusively ours for the weekend.

## Getting There

Driving is the primary way to get to Camp Whitman. It's an easy drive from most of upstate New York and the surrounding states.

**Nearest airports:**

- **ITH** (Ithaca Tompkins), ~1 hour drive
- **ROC** (Rochester), ~1.5 hours drive
- **SYR** (Syracuse), ~1.5 hours drive

## Bus/Train/Carpool

Join our [Telegram chat](https://t.me/fingerlakesanthroweekend) to ask questions and coordinate carpools.

## Parking

- Park in designated areas only
- Follow staff directions on arrival
- No parking near cabins except for loading and unloading
